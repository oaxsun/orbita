DRKPRTY v9 fixes

- Public site now uses robust publication logic:
  - published true/string true accepted.
  - scheduled articles with publishAt due are shown.
  - future publishAt stays hidden.
- Admin now automatically flips due scheduled articles to published:true in Firestore when loaded.
- Save article forces published:true if publishAt time is already due.
- Hero controls now have direct fallback handlers:
  - Agregar destacada
  - Seleccionar destacada
  - Elegir artículo
  - Remove featured
- Removed the over-aggressive v8 capture delegation that could block hero controls.
